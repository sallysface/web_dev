House M.D. follows a doctor with a limp, and his team of colleuges. It is both a show about medical related topics, as well as interpersonal affairs, covering topics of addiction, relationships, and mental health. House is a hilarious show, that also provides plenty of drama to keep one entertained throughout all 8 seasons.

My Favorite Characters

  • Gregory House, The main character of the show, a brilliant but misanthropic diagnostician.
  • Lisa Cuddy, The hospital administrator who often clashes with House but cares deeply for her patients.
  • James Wilson, House's best friend and an oncologist, providing a moral compass for House.
  • Remmy "Thirteen" Hadley, A complex character dealing with personal struggles while being a skilled doctor.
  • Allison Cameron, An empathetic immunologist who often challenges House's methods.
  • Juan "Alvie" Alverez, A man House meets during his time in rehab, who brings a unique perspective to his life.
